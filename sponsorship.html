Fur Ball Gala Partnership Opportunities

We invite you to consider a sponsorship package for one of San Diego’s most creative and beloved events, San Diego Humane Society’s Fur Ball gala. This unique event has been voted San Diego’s best gala by Ranch and Coast magazine and provides an innovative and engaging way to reach a dedicated base of customers – animal lovers. Or, you might consider a Fur Ball sponsorship as a creative way to reward your customers, employees or partners while demonstrating your commitment to animals. Either way, the Fur Ball is an excellent opportunity to associate your brand name with an organization that has universal appeal.

Connecting With Passionate Animal Lovers

San Diego Humane Society’s annual Fur Ball is the most highly anticipated, ‘must-attend’ event of the season. Now in its 29th year, the Fur Ball gala is attended by 700 passionate and engaged animal lovers from throughout San Diego County. Well-dressed animals and their pet parents enjoy a night of fun and festivities under the stars while helping to support the programs and services of San Diego Humane Society.

A Fur Ball sponsorship allows you to increase your business exposure among a highly-influential audience. It can enhance your customer base while showing your dedication to the animals and our community. This event provides a special opportunity to expose your brand in an intimate and unique setting with hundreds of potential customers.

Event Profile

Fur Ball is a very special evening for animals, their pet parents and San Diego’s social elite. The event includes a cocktail reception, wine raffle, live and silent auctions, entertainment and dancing. A touching program celebrating the animals and programs of San Diego Humane Society accompanies a gourmet meal prepared by renowned chef and owner of Pamplemousse Grille, Jeffrey Strauss. This delicious meal is paired with fine wines.
