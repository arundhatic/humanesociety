2018 Fur Ball

Saturday, Oct. 6, 2018

The annual Fur Ball event organised by San Diego Humane Society is to raise funds to support animals and different programs of Humane Society.

These funds allow them to maintain critical programs and services that helps nearly 30,000 pets and wildlife receive the second chance they deserve through rescue, rehabilitation, adoption – and so much more.

An event that elicits support of guests, sponsors, volunteers, media partners, and everyone else who can help make it a successful event.
